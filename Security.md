## How to Use It

### Basic Circle

1. Open the app
2. Enter your desired diameter (example: 25)
3. The grid shows you each row from top to bottom
4. In Minecraft, place blocks exactly where the grid shows
5. Repeat for each row until your circle is complete

### Hollow vs Solid

- **Hollow** → Just the outline of the circle (good for walls, towers)
- **Solid** → Every block inside the circle (good for floors, platforms)

### Wall vs Floor

- **Floor Mode** → Circle lies flat on the ground
- **Wall Mode** → Circle stands vertically (for archways, tunnels)
